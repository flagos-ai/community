# Planned SIGs

This directory contains draft charters for SIGs that have not yet been activated.

## Activation Criteria

A planned SIG may be activated as a formal SIG when the following conditions are met:
1. At least 1 Chair confirmed (target: ≥2)
2. At least 1 Tech Lead confirmed
3. At least 3 initial members
